## Visual Hierarchy

**Primary Focal Point:** The first large image card (span-2 or span-4) in the top-left area of the Bento Grid draws the eye first.

**Visual Priority Order:**
1. Large image cards (span-2/4) — highest visual weight
2. Dark variant cards — contrast against light background
3. Stats card with live data — dynamic content
4. Text cards (light variant) — supporting content
5. Terminal cards — niche aesthetic accent

## Animations & Interactions

### Required Hover Effects

| Effect | From | To | Easing |
|--------|------|-----|--------|
| Card background shift (light) | #F7F7F7 | #F0F0F0 | cubic-bezier |
| Arrow icon nudge | translate(0, 0) | translate(4px, -4px) | cubic-bezier |
| Image grayscale-to-color | grayscale(100%) | grayscale(0%) + scale(1.02) | cubic-bezier |
| Cursor blink | visible | hidden | keyframe |

### Transition Timing

- Use cubic-bezier easing for all transitions
- Standard duration: 200-300ms (planner's discretion)
